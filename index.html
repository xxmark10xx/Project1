Winner:

This game is played with two players, player 1 (blue) on the left and plyaer 2 (red) on the right. You each start with one button on the top and bottom. the objective is to click on the opposite players button to add to there hand. For example, when player 1 starts they hit player 2's button either one and it adds a button to player 2. Then player 2 can choose between his two hands (1 button or 2) and add that to player 1's side. once a players hand reaches 5 they lose that hand. The last person with a hand still out wins.
